Robert had a home built next door to the family home (5508 Hadden). He lived their many years.  Had two wives and one child.  Divorced first wife and remarried.  First wife continued to live with them. Then divorced the second wife and remarried his first wife (Connie). Then the second wife remarried and the three of them lived together.


In 1935 lived at 2443 N. Kedzie, Chicago Illinois.
