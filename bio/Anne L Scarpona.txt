Anne L. Onder (nee Scarpona)

Anne L. (Scarpona) Onder was born 1913 in Savannah, GA. Her parents were, Natale and Francis Scarpona. She moved to Ogdensburg, N.J. with her family. She was 14 at the time. That was in 1927. In 1931, at the age of 18 she was married to James J. Onder of Ogdensburg, N.J. Living there until she retired in 1977. In 1961, Anne started working for the Accurate Forming Corp., in Hamburg, N.J. She worked for them for 16 yrs. before retiring and moving to Port Jervis, N.J. She was a parishioner of St. James R.C. Church in Montaque, N.J.
